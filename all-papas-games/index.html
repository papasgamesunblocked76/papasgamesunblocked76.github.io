About Papa's Games Series

The Papa Louie series is a popular collection of platforming and time management video games created by Flipline Studios. The franchise began in 2006 with Papa Louie: When Pizzas Attack!, which was initially released as a free-to-play Flash game on November 9, 2006.

Over the years, the series expanded to include numerous restaurant simulation games where players take on the role of employees at various food establishments owned by Papa Louie. Each game focuses on a different type of food service, from pizzerias to bakeries, with players taking customer orders, preparing food, and serving customers to earn points and tips.

Main Game Mechanics

  • Taking customer orders with specific preferences
  • Preparing food through various minigame stations
  • Serving customers and earning tips based on performance
  • Upgrading your restaurant with new equipment
  • Unlocking new ingredients and recipes

Popular Games in the Series

  • Papa's Pizzeria (2007)
  • Papa's Burgeria (2010)
  • Papa's Freezeria (2011)
  • Papa's Pancakeria (2012)
  • Papa's Hot Doggeria (2012)
  • Papa's Cupcakeria (2013)
  • Papa's Donuteria (2014)

Following the discontinuation of Adobe Flash in December 2020, many of the classic Papa's games were remastered for mobile devices (under the "To Go!" subtitle) and later for Steam as "Deluxe" editions. This has allowed these beloved games to continue being played by fans worldwide.

Papa's Games are recognized for their addictive gameplay, colorful graphics, and the satisfaction of creating perfect food items to please virtual customers. The series has garnered a dedicated following and continues to be popular among casual gamers of all ages.
