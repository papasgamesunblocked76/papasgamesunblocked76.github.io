Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2025

At Papa's Games Unblocked, we prioritize the protection of your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Game progress and preferences
  • Basic device information for game optimization
  • Anonymous usage statistics to improve our services

How We Use Your Information

  • To save your game progress
  • To optimize game performance
  • To improve our services based on anonymous usage data
  • To maintain the security of our platform

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for all ages, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us for immediate removal.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services for analytics and performance optimization. These services may collect anonymous usage data subject to their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your saved game data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.
